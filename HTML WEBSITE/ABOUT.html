Nullscape

About




Nullscape is a passion project, dedicated to helping people learn to make games.

Our mission statement is to inspire, enable and create the next generation of gamebuilders.





What we offer

Nullscape offers informative information to enable and teach people how to create games, step by step with a database of profesional sources.

We have a collection of well known sources that many coders use, making it easy to find help with issues.

A collection of video tutorials that teach simple coding, along with links to teachers.

Advice from profesional programmers and gamemakers, explaining the concepts and theory to games.




History

Nullscape was founded by a passionate programmer, who wanted to help and teach the youth

Founded in 2024, Nullscape was build by the group up, using the knowldge of the programmer and then later others to create a information website

Our founder payed with his own money to host the website, untill ads where able to be added allowing the website to flourish
